ABOUT

Cyber Arcade is a cloud gaming service developed and operated by the Cyber Company. It is capable of streaming video games to players up to 4K resolution at 60 frames per second with support for high-dynamic-range (HDR) via the company's numerous data centers. This allows the users to play several video games on their browser, without any need to download them. We have a large collection of upto 600,000 games which makes it possible for our customers to play any game they want. By providing such a large collection for such a discounted price, we have managed to become behemeths in the gaming industry.
